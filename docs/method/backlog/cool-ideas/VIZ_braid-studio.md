# Braid Studio

Instead of a traditional conflict-resolution UI, build a `Braid Studio`
surface in `warp-ttd`.

The user would inspect:

- the two or more strands
- their shared precursor
- the braid over those strands
- obstruction witnesses
- possible collapse targets

Why this matters:

- It treats unresolved plurality as a first-class object.
- It turns merge into causal inspection and collapse, not text surgery.
- It is the most direct operational expression of strand/braid theory.
