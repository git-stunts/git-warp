# NO_DOGS_NO_MASTERS

Historical umbrella legend retained for old references.

`NO_DOGS_NO_MASTERS` / `NDNM` used to lump together two distinct
failure modes:

- ownership failures such as god objects and mixed-concern facades
- runtime-model failures such as typedef corridors and phantom types

Those are now tracked separately by the live invariant legends:

- `OWN` for ownership and cohesion
- `MODEL` for runtime-backed forms and constructor invariants
- `CAST` when the “solution” was a lie instead of a model

`NDNM` remains useful as historical language in older docs, but it is
not a live backlog legend for new `bad-code/` work.
